Welcome to Node Bucket!

Node Bucket is here to make your life easier!

Do you have a honey-to-do list?

Are you always going to the store and forgetting things?

How about kids in sports, getting one child from here to there?

If you answered yes to any of these questions, than NodeBucket was made for you!

What are able to do with our app? Well below I have explained it all!

  • Create tasks - you know those task you give your husband but he never seems to complete. Or the one those teenagers forget about.
  • Sort tasks by importance, put the highest priority first!
  • When one task is completed, drag it and drop it to the done column.
  • You can also move that done task back to the todo list if needed.
  • Don't like the list? That's ok, you can delete those task you want to get rid of.

This app just might become your new best friend!
